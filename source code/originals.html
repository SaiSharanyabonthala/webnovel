AGE MATTERS

Age Matters is a romantic comedy webtoon by Enjelicious, available on WEBTOON. It follows Rose Choi, a 29-year-old woman who unexpectedly becomes the personal chef of Daniel Yoon, a 23-year-old tech CEO. Despite their age gap and clashing personalities, their relationship slowly grows from awkward strangers to something more meaningful. The story is full of humor, heart, and themes of love, career, and self-growth.

SAVE ME

Save Me is a webtoon created by Big Hit Entertainment and LICO, based on the BTS Universe (BU). It follows Kim Seokjin, who finds himself trapped in a time loop, forced to relive the same day—April 11—over and over. His mission is to save his six friends (based on BTS members) from tragic fates. Each friend is struggling with their own pain, and Seokjin must figure out how to change the future and break the loop.
