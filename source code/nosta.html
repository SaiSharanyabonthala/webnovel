Monday

Death Note Death Note is a critically acclaimed Japanese anime and manga series created by writer Tsugumi Ohba and illustrator Takeshi Obata. The story centers around Light Yagami, a brilliant high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook called the "Death Note," which grants the power to kill anyone whose name is written in it, as long as the writer knows their face. With ambitions to rid the world of evil and become a god-like figure, Light begins a moral crusade under the alias "Kira." However, his actions draw the attention of a genius detective known only as "L," leading to an intense psychological battle of wits. Death Note is widely praised for its dark themes, suspenseful storytelling, and ethical dilemmas, making it one of the most iconic series in the anime and manga world.

Tuesday

Demon Slayer Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Koyoharu Gotouge. Set in Taishō-era Japan, the story follows a kind-hearted boy named Tanjiro Kamado who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his younger sister, Nezuko, is turned into one. The series beautifully blends action, emotion, and supernatural elements as Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps to seek a cure for Nezuko and take revenge on the powerful demon responsible. Praised for its breathtaking animation by Ufotable, especially in its fight scenes, Demon Slayer has become a global phenomenon, with both its manga and anime breaking records and winning hearts worldwide.

Wednesday

Naruto Naruto is a legendary Japanese anime and manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a spirited and determined ninja who dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the title of Hokage—the leader of his village, the Hidden Leaf. Despite being shunned by others because of the powerful Nine-Tails Fox sealed within him, Naruto works tirelessly to gain respect, make friends, and protect those he cares about. The series explores themes of perseverance, friendship, identity, and the meaning of true strength. With a massive cast of unique characters, emotional storytelling, and epic battles, Naruto became one of the "Big Three" anime alongside One Piece and Bleach, and has left a lasting impact on anime fans around the world.

Thursday

One piece One Piece is a long-running and beloved Japanese anime and manga series created by Eiichiro Oda. The story follows Monkey D. Luffy, a cheerful and fearless boy with the ability to stretch like rubber after eating a mystical Devil Fruit. Inspired by his childhood hero, Luffy sets out on an adventurous journey across dangerous seas to find the legendary treasure known as the “One Piece” and become the King of the Pirates. Along the way, he gathers a loyal crew called the Straw Hat Pirates, each with their own dreams and backstories. Known for its imaginative world-building, emotional storytelling, and themes of friendship, freedom, and chasing your dreams, One Piece has become the best-selling manga of all time and one of the most iconic anime series in history.

Friday

True Beauty is a popular South Korean webtoon created by Yaongyi. It follows the story of Lim Ju-kyung, a high school girl who becomes famous at school for her stunning looks—thanks to her amazing makeup skills. However, she hides her bare face and fears people discovering her real appearance. The webtoon explores beauty standards, self-confidence, and the journey of embracing one’s true self, all with a blend of drama, romance, and humor.

Saturday

Summertime Rendering is a Japanese manga series by Yasuki Tanaka. The story follows Shinpei Ajiro, who returns to his hometown on Hitogashima Island after the death of his childhood friend, Ushio Kofune. As he investigates the mysterious circumstances surrounding Ushio's death, Shinpei uncovers dark secrets and faces supernatural phenomena. The manga was serialized from October 2017 to February 2021 and has been adapted into an anime series.

7FATES: CHAKHO is a collaborative urban fantasy webtoon developed by HYBE in partnership with the global sensation BTS. Launched in January 2022 on the WEBTOON platform, the series draws inspiration from Korea's Joseon Dynasty tiger hunters, known as "Chakhogapsa," and reimagines traditional folklore within a futuristic, cyberpunk setting.
